About NowZNews

NowZNews is a neon Gen-Z news outlet that uses AI automation to scan, rank, and broadcast the biggest stories in World, Tech, and US/Canada news.

What We Do

Our system:

Our Mission

We want to give Gen-Z and young audiences the most important stories in a format that fits today’s attention span: short, visual, and honest.
