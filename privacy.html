Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how NowZNews uses information when you connect your TikTok account and when our automation creates and posts content on your behalf. This document is primarily for TikTok Developer review and applies to our sandbox integration.

1. Data we receive

When you log in with TikTok, we may receive the following information, depending on the scopes you approve:

  • Basic profile information (for example: open ID, display name, avatar) via user.info.basic.
  • Video upload and publishing status (IDs, upload status, processing status) when we call the Content Posting API.

2. How we use this data

  • To authenticate you and link your TikTok account to the NowZNews automation.
  • To upload videos that our workflow generates (news clips) to your TikTok account.
  • To check whether a video upload or publish request was successful.

We do not use this data to build advertising profiles, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data storage

  • Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored securely in our automation environment and are used only to call TikTok APIs you approved.
  • Log data (for example: upload status, error codes, log IDs) may be stored for debugging and monitoring.
  • Generated media files (videos and audio) may be stored locally for processing before they are uploaded to TikTok.

4. Sharing of data

We do not share your TikTok data with unrelated third parties. Data is shared only with:

  • TikTok, when making API requests you authorized.
  • Service providers we use to host or run our automation tools (for example, task runners or storage providers), strictly for operating the service.

5. Your choices

  • You can revoke our access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok account settings.
  • You may request that we delete locally stored logs or media that are associated with your account, subject to any legal requirements.
